# Homework 2: Create Logic! 🧠

## 📝 Problem Statement
Write a program that reads 3 integers representing a classroom:
- `nb`: Number of boys
- `ng`: Number of girls
- `nt`: Number of teachers

Translate the following English sentences into C++ boolean expressions and print their results (1 for True, 0 for False):
1. `nb` greater than 25 >
2. `ng` less than or equal to 30 <=
3. `nb` > 20 and `nt` > 2 or `ng` > 30 and `nt` > 4 
4. Either `nb` < 60 or `ng` < 70
5. Neither `nb` >= 60 nor `ng` >= 70
6. `nb` is exactly 10 more students than `ng`
7. Difference between `nb` and `ng` is more than 10 or `nt` > 5
8. Either `nb` is 10 more students than `ng` or `ng` is 15 more students than `nb`
